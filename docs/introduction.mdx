## What is Magpipe?

Magpipe is a complete AI-powered business phone system that handles calls, texts, and website chat for your business. Deploy intelligent AI agents that answer calls 24/7, respond to SMS messages, book appointments, and provide customer support - all while maintaining your brand identity.

## Platform Features

### AI Phone Agents
Create custom AI agents with unique personalities, voices, and knowledge. Each agent can:
- Answer inbound calls with natural conversation
- Make outbound calls for appointments, follow-ups, and sales
- Respond to SMS and MMS messages intelligently
- Chat with website visitors in real-time
- Book appointments via calendar integrations
- Transfer calls to humans when needed
- Access your knowledge base for accurate answers

### Multi-Channel Communication
- **Voice Calls**: HD voice with AI-powered conversation
- **SMS/MMS**: Two-way texting with image support
- **Web Chat**: Embeddable widget for your website
- **Unified Inbox**: All conversations in one place

### Mobile Companion App
Magpipe is built as a Progressive Web App (PWA), giving you a native app experience on any device:
- **Install to Home Screen**: Add to your phone like a native app
- **Works Everywhere**: Same experience on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Personal Companion**: Monitor calls, reply to messages, and manage your agents on the go
- **Push Notifications**: Get alerted to new calls and messages instantly
- **Offline Access**: View recent conversations even without internet

On mobile, Magpipe becomes your personal AI assistant companion - check your inbox, listen to call recordings, and take over conversations from anywhere.

### White Label Ready
Magpipe is designed for agencies and resellers:
- Custom branding and domain
- Remove all Magpipe branding
- Your logo, colors, and identity
- Client-facing portal
- Per-client billing and management

### Team Collaboration
- **Organizations**: Create teams with multiple members
- **Role-Based Access**: Owner, Admin, and Member roles
- **Shared Resources**: Agents, numbers, and contacts across team
- **Invitation System**: Email-based team invitations

### Enterprise Features
- **Multiple Agents**: Create specialized agents for different purposes
- **External SIP Trunks**: Bring your own carrier for telephony
- **Knowledge Base**: RAG-powered contextual AI responses
- **40+ Apps**: Connect to your existing tools via MCP
- **API Access**: Full REST API for custom integrations
- **Webhooks**: Real-time event notifications

## What is Solo Mobile?

Solo Mobile is an AI-powered phone assistant that handles calls and messages for your business. Deploy intelligent AI agents to answer calls, send texts, and chat with customers - all from one platform.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="robot" title="AI Agents">
    Create custom AI agents with unique personalities, voices, and knowledge. Each agent can handle calls, respond to texts, and chat with website visitors.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="phone" title="Phone Numbers">
    Provision local or toll-free numbers. Assign agents to numbers to automatically handle inbound calls and SMS.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="inbox" title="Unified Inbox">
    View all calls, texts, and chat conversations in one place. See transcripts, listen to recordings, and reply to messages.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="book" title="Knowledge Base">
    Add websites, documents, and custom content to give your agents context about your business.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="message" title="Chat Widget">
    Embed a chat widget on your website. Visitors can chat with your AI agent in real-time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="plug" title="Integrations">
    Connect to HubSpot, Google Calendar, and other tools. Sync contacts and let agents book appointments.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
